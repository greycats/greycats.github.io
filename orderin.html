The Story

OrderIn is the #1 food delivery app in South Africa. OrderIn operates in South Africa's major cities and allows users to quickly have their food delivered from their favorite restaurants.

The Challenge

Orderin returned to Interactive Labs with a new project of overhauling OrderIn iOS app to better server the changing online food delivery market . Worked closely with stakeholders and users, we help Orderin optimize the ordering experience. Simplified the group ordering process significantly to increase revenues via this core use case.

Solutions

From website to native app

The initial information came in the form of traditional website.

To get it into a form that we could use in an app, were going to to need to create a set of hi-fidelity wireframe. We created wireframes that mirrored the agreed-upon flow and then attacking from multiple angles to try to find the best experience to order food from iOS devices

Smart Filter

New and improved Orderin app allows users to filter their options by price, cuisine and dietary, which makes it so much easier for users to make their everyday torn decision of what to order today.

Delightful onboarding flow

With the new interface, we knew we couldn't expect an existing user to understand the new features without any sort of direction.

With our users in mind, we created a proper onboarding that can ease users in our new flow and help them pick their location for delivery quicker and more accurate using the latest google map function.
