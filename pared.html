The Story

A labor marketplace for restaurants. A real-time operations and logistics platform that solves the pain for restaurants and puts money in workers bank accounts. The app is like uber for restaurant that pairs restaurants with on-demand workers, enable restaurants to quickly, efficiently and cost-effectively hire trained staff.

The Challenge

Pared's vision is to create a solution to help the restaurant industry by solving staffing issues for restauranteurs while giving cooks the ability to earn more money and gain experience. With two applications Pared Work for restaurants and Pare Pros for professional with specific skill sets that match restaurant's needs, we help Pared create a platform that makes restaurant life easier.

Solutions

Pared Pro.

Help restaurant pros find the perfect gigs

Never miss a opportunity

With Pared Pro, restaurant workers can see a list of future gigs which they were offered and have accepted (by matching their skills with required skills from the restaurant.

Users can quickly scan latest gigs, working time, ETA, Job type, restaurant info, which makes it so much easier for them to keep up with restaurant job market.

Advanced search function

Advanced filters by roles, pay rates, urgency, distance to restaurant help workers sort and find the best gigs which match their skill sets and offers the best rate.

Favorite workplace

Pared put the power in the hand of restaurant workers and let them curate a personalized list of their favorite restaurants by post-shift review. this allows users to privately pick out restaurants that they want to come back to later.

Availability settings

Users now have full control of their schedule and are able to balance their life. Pared allows users to set their available time by simply switching on/off the timeframes. Notifications from shifts only appear in their available time.

Pared Work.

A perfect staffing solution for restaurant owner

Total control of staffing

Pared wants to approach a different design in the restaurant app. The calendar view is the main entry point where restaurant owners can easily see shifts on a specific date, create new shifts and allow multiple date booking.

To ensure a high rate of gig-booking as well as flexibility for staffing activity, we proposed a bar showing the estimated rate for the shift based on roles and time of shift. If the shift is too urgent, restaurant owners can use a slider to increase the rate to encourage workers to take the gigs.

Favourited pros by location

Like the Worker App, Let restaurant curate a personalized list of favorite pros. This is an on/off choice. The list can be sorted by role, date of work. It allows restaurants to privately pick out pros that they want to work with later.
